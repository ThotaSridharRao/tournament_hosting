Important Rules & Information

Essential guidelines and information for tournament participants.

  • • Registration: Teams can register only once for this tournament. Make sure all team details are correct before submitting.
  • • Match Schedule: Check the Brackets page for your match times and opponents. All match schedules are updated in real-time.
  • • Room Details: Room ID and password will be shared on the Brackets page at your scheduled match time. Be ready 10 minutes before your match.
