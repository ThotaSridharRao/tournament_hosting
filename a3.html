How to Register as a Host on Uni Games

December 14, 2024 10 min read Uni Games Team

Ready to take your passion for gaming to the next level? Becoming a tournament host on Uni Games allows you to organize exciting competitive events, build communities, and earn substantial revenue. This comprehensive guide will walk you through every step of the host registration process, payment systems, and the powerful features available to hosts.

Why Become a Host on Uni Games?

Hosting tournaments on Uni Games offers exceptional opportunities:

  • Earn Substantial Revenue: Keep 90% of tournament entry fees with transparent payout system
  • Professional Tools: Access advanced tournament management, bracket generation, and analytics
  • Build Community: Create lasting connections with gamers and establish your esports brand
  • Flexible Operations: Host events on your schedule with full control over formats and rules
  • Multi-Game Support: Organize tournaments for BGMI, Valorant, CS2, FIFA, and more
  • Automated Systems: Streamlined participant management, payment processing, and dispute resolution

Host Registration Requirements

To ensure platform integrity and legal compliance, hosts must provide:

  • Identity Verification: Valid Aadhaar card (12-digit number + document upload)
  • Contact Information: Phone number, email, and organization/company name
  • Banking Details: Account information for automated payouts (added after approval)
  • Technical Requirements: Reliable internet connection and device for tournament management
  • Agreement Compliance: Accept terms of service and verification requirements

Step-by-Step Registration Process

1

Access Host Registration

Navigate to the host registration page directly or click "Become a Host" from your user dashboard. The registration form is separate from regular player accounts and requires additional verification.

[Screenshot: Host Registration Page Access]
2

Complete Personal Information

Fill out the comprehensive registration form with your username, email, secure password, organization/company name, and contact phone number. All fields are mandatory for verification purposes.

[Screenshot: Personal Information Form Fields]
3

Aadhaar Verification Process

Enter your 12-digit Aadhaar number and upload a clear photo or scan of your Aadhaar card (PDF, JPG, JPEG, or PNG format, max 5MB). This is mandatory for Indian legal compliance and ensures platform security.

[Screenshot: Aadhaar Verification Section]
4

Accept Terms and Submit

Review and accept the terms of service and verification agreement. These cover platform rules, payout policies, and host responsibilities. Submit your application for review by our verification team.

[Screenshot: Terms Agreement and Submit Button]

The host registration process is straightforward and designed to get you started quickly. Once your application is submitted, our team will review it within 24-48 hours. Upon approval, you'll receive access to the host dashboard where you can immediately begin creating and managing tournaments.

Ready to start hosting tournaments and building your gaming community? Complete the registration process today and join the growing network of successful tournament hosts on Uni Games.
